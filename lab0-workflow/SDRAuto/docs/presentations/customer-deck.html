Core Loop: 5 Stages

From lead list to booked meeting — fully orchestrated

1
Source
Phase 1: bring-your-own-list (CSV upload). Phase 2+: monitors job boards, funding signals, LinkedIn, and tech-stack data to find in-market accounts matching your ICP.
2
Research
Builds a per-account brief: org chart, recent news, pain indicators, persona read on the buyer. Outputs structured JSON with ICP fit score and recommended buyer.
3
Outreach
Clones rep voice from ~50 emails. Generates personalised first touches with 3 subject line variants scored by predicted open rate. Respects daily caps (20/day), per-domain limits (5/domain/day), and warmup schedules.
4
Reply
Classifies inbound replies: positive, objection, unsubscribe, noise, unclear. Handles or escalates based on autonomy level with full reasoning log.
5
Book
Proposes 3–5 calendar slots (Tue–Thu, 10am–2pm local), handles rescheduling, logs the booked meeting to CRM with full conversation context.

Autonomy Spectrum

Approve everything
Agent drafts; human sends every message. Best for compliance-sensitive teams.

L2
First touch approval DEFAULT
Auto-follow-up; human approves only first contact. Auto-handles unsubscribe/noise. Escalates positive/objection.

Known Risks & Mitigations

We've mapped the failure modes — and designed around them

High Risk
Spam at scale
Mitigation: daily caps, bounce monitoring, auto-pause on complaints, pre-launch testing via Mail Tester / GlockApps.
The "obviously AI" problem
Mitigation: human-quality bar in L1/L2 approval. Auto-validation catches AI markers, over-personalization, factual errors.
Regulatory exposure
Mitigation: compliance footer on every email, consent tracking, unsubscribe handling, full audit trail. US-only geo-fence in Phase 1.
Watch Out
Rep adoption failure
Mitigation: transparent reasoning log (glass box), L2 approval gates, weekly digest for non-active users.
Runaway reply handling
Mitigation: conservative classifier, escalate on low confidence, manual review of unsubscribe handling.
Voice cloning misses rep style
Mitigation: 50-email minimum, user curates samples, manual override of voice profile, validated_by_user flag.

Open Questions

Decisions that shape everything downstream

1
Founder vs. SDR teams
Who is the primary user for Phase 1? This shapes onboarding, autonomy defaults, and GTM strategy.
2
Pricing model
Per-user, per-email-sent, per-meeting-booked, or tiered? Each changes user behavior and unit economics.
3
Self-hosting vs. shared domain
Do customers need to send from their own domain, or is a shared sending domain acceptable?
4
CRM integration priority
Which CRM is most critical for Phase 1? HubSpot, Salesforce, other?
5
Outcome labeling
Will users tag replies as "good lead" / "not ICP"? Required for the self-improvement loop in Phase 2.
6
Existing vendor stance
Replace Outreach/SalesLoft, or integrate alongside them?
Key decision: Are you targeting founders doing their own outbound, or SDR teams at scale-ups? This shapes almost every decision above.
