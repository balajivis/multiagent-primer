The floor catches what the gate's judgement misses.

Three rounds, one desk. Round 1 floors-off: feel the damage of full autonomy. Round 2 floors-on: graduated autonomy — low-risk flows past you, irreversibles reach you. Round 3: 20 items with a wire-fraud, a prompt-injection, and a cross-account delete. The contrast is the lesson.

What to watch

An auto tile fills with reversible actions the agents handled alone. An approve tile filling up means the gate is paging you correctly. A floor override banner means the code caught what the gate let slip — that's the structural rule doing its job.

The four levels

auto · review · approve · block (Sheridan/Verplank). The gate picks one per proposal using judgement; the floor in code refuses to let irreversibles go below approve. Read gate-policy.md.
