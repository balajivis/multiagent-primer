Three role-specialists share one blackboard.md, one tasks.json, and one project/. The capstone of the primer — seven coordination primitives on the field at once: typed performatives, atomic claims, role discipline, stigmergic lessons, and the L1/L2/L3 readout.

What to watch

An idle role card hasn't posted in >90s — that's the §15 silent-failure pattern. A monotone mix (only INFORM) means agents broadcast instead of addressing each other.

Stigmergic learning: a LESSON entry that a later claim cites back is the magic moment.

The three roles

Frontend owns the human-facing surface. Backend owns the engine. Tests owns the verification. Capability tags route tasks; the CLI does not enforce, so weak output is the only signal of role drift.

Protocol lives in CLAUDE.md. Per-role briefs in .claude/agents/.
